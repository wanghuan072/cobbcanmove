# COBB CAN MOVE — Player Hub (cobbcanmove.site)

**Live site:** [https://cobbcanmove.site/](https://cobbcanmove.site/)

**COBB CAN MOVE** is a top-down survival horror experience: you move through a dark dungeon, keep **coal-fed light** alive, complete objectives such as **furnaces** and **breakers**, and try to extract before **Cobb** changes the rules. **Story Mode** teaches authored beats; **Endless Mode** keeps the pressure high with heavier randomization. This hub at **cobbcanmove.site** is a browser-first portal: play the flagship game, read guides, browse curated titles, and use moderated comments—not a replacement for official storefront pages when we link out (for example itch.io).

---

## What you are playing

- **Perspective & tone:** Top-down pixel survival horror with readable tension—sound, sightlines, and tempo matter as much as raw reflexes.
- **Core loop:** Light the path → finish the floor’s jobs → route out before mistakes stack.
- **Coal & light:** Coal feeds your light radius; running empty is not always instant failure, but it is when the map stops forgiving sloppy movement.
- **Cobb:** The hunter’s behavior **rotates** (hearing, smell, sight, reach, speed, duplication-style pressure, and similar beats depending on the build). Treat each floor as a new contract, not a repeat of the last route autopilot.

## Quick guide (before your first run)

1. **Controls (typical browser build):** Move with **WASD** or arrows; interact with **E** or **Space** when the UI prompts. Click inside the embed if inputs feel dead.
2. **Noise & information:** Sprinting and careless interacts broadcast position—slow hands often survive longer than panic rolls.
3. **Objectives first:** Finish what the floor asks (furnaces, breakers, etc.) before you chase optional loot unless you know the route cold.
4. **Read the rule strip:** When patches retune floors, trust what the client shows over old muscle memory from a previous version.
5. **Network:** If the embed fails on a strict network, try another connection or open the player in a fresh tab after the page loads.

For longer reads, open the **[Blog](https://cobbcanmove.site/blog)**—articles such as *how to play* and *Story floors / Cobb rules* go deeper than this README.
